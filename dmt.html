DMT, the myth of the pineal gland and the potential role of gut flora

DMT is sometimes referred to as "the spirit molecule". It is said that DMT is released when people die, and other such things. I have no opinion on this and have never taken DMT. This page is an attempt to point to a new and unexplored avenue of research into endogenous DMT production in humans that lightly touches on chemtrails and the deterioration of spirituality in the developed world

Dead Ends

There has been a lot of ink spilled (and pixels lit up) over the idea that DMT is produced in the pineal gland in humans. As far as I can tell, these efforts have been unsuccessful. So much so, that even talking about the 'pineal gland' these days marks a person as a loon or conspiracy theorist.

Alternatives

I want to sketch out an alternative pathway for DMT research that lightly touches on a notion of chemtrails.

  • The herbicide Glyphosate is generally considered safe to humans because the metabolic pathway in plants that it disrupts, the Shikimate Pathway isn't present in humans
  • Plants produce DMT through a metabolic process that relies on/is related to the Shikimate Pathway
  • There is research to suggest that Glyphosate is toxic to some human gut flora, i.e. they use the Shikimate Pathway
This immediately raises two possibilities:
  1. Why should human gut flora not also produce DMT through similar metabolic pathways as plants
  2. If this is the case, would food contaminated with Glyphosate disrupt the production of DMT by human gut flora
If 1. and 2. above turn out to be true, then the rise of Glyphosate as a herbicide and therefore the contamination of food with it could be preventing the otherwise 'natural' microdosing of DMT that would be occurring in human beings. From there, the sky's the limit in terms of what effect this might have: clearly it's not hard to imagine the human experience would be quite different if we were constantly microdosing hallucinogens.

In my view, research should be urgently focussing on whether DMT-vulnerable gut flora naturally found in human beings produce endogenous DMT and whether this can be absorbed by humans.

On Chemtrails

As an aside, the above theories as to the effects of Glyphosate allow this herbicide to meet the basic notions of a chemtrail.

  • It is sprayed on plants (occasionally from the air)
  • It reduces/eliminates endogenous DMT production
  • This makes humans less spiritual, more prosaic, and dare I say it probably more obedient
